The Shawshank Redemption

Description:

This movie is a drama set in the 1940s about a banker named Andy Dufresne, who is wrongly convicted of murder and sent to the Shawshank State Penitentiary. There, he befriends a fellow inmate, Red, and finds ways to live and improve life within the prison. The story is about hope, resilience, and the enduring power of friendship.
